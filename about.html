About

I am a multidisciplinary artist with my deepest roots in music, writing and embodied expressions. I started dancing, playing piano and telling stories at an early age, later moving on to explore other art forms, like theatre and performance art.

I move between different media for my creative expressions. I like experimenting. I don't like conforming too much to norms and conventions. I work on my own and in collaborations with others. I appreciate the artistic adventure of presence and exploration.

Over the last period of time, my greatest focus has been on making music.
